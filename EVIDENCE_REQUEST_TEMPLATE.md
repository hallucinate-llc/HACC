# Evidence-Request Template for DEI/Discrimination Compliance Audit

**TO:** [Organization/Department Name]  
**FROM:** Housing Authority Compliance Audit Team  
**DATE:** [Current Date]  
**RE:** Request for Documentation — Diversity, Equity, Inclusion (DEI) and Antidiscrimination Compliance Review

---

## Purpose

We are conducting a comprehensive audit of policies, programs, contracts, and practices to ensure compliance with the Attorney General Bondi Memo (July 29, 2025): "Guidance for Recipients of Federal Funding Regarding Unlawful Discrimination."

This memo applies to all entities receiving federal funding, including state agencies, local governments, and their contractors. We are requesting documentation to assess compliance with federal antidiscrimination laws (Title VI, Title VII, Title IX, Equal Protection Clause).

---

## What We're Looking For

We need evidence in the following categories:

### Category 1: Governance Policies & Handbooks
- Employee handbooks (current and last 3 years)
- Procurement manuals and contract templates
- HR policies (hiring, promotion, discipline, retaliation)
- Anti-discrimination and anti-retaliation policies
- Diversity, Equity, Inclusion (DEI) policies or plans
- Board/Council resolutions or directives related to diversity or equity
- Any "model policies" adopted from the state or federal sources

**Submit:**
- PDF or Word docs, with date and department of origin

---

### Category 2: Position Descriptions & Job Postings
- Last 20 job postings or announcements (any position type)
- Position descriptions with selection criteria
- Any language about "underrepresented groups," "diversity," "lived experience," or "cultural competence" as selection criteria
- Interview scorecards or evaluation rubrics

**Submit:**
- PDFs or Word docs; note department and posting date

---

### Category 3: Hiring & Selection Records
- Sample applicant pools (race/ethnicity/sex data if tracked)
- Candidate shortlists or "diverse slates"
- Interview notes or scoring records (sample of 5–10 recent hires)
- Promotion records with selection rationale
- Any documented instances where demographic diversity was a stated factor in selection

**Submit:**
- Redacted to protect individual privacy; aggregate summaries acceptable

---

### Category 4: Procurement & Contracting
- Last 3 years of RFPs (Requests for Proposal) or RFBs (Requests for Bid)
- Bid evaluations or scoring matrices
- Contract awards memos or decisions
- Any MWBE (Minority/Women-Owned Business Enterprise) set-asides or preferences
- Vendor/contractor diversity requirements or monitoring language
- Executed contracts that reference diversity or equity goals

**Submit:**
- PDFs or Word docs; mark sensitive sections as requested

---

### Category 5: Training & Development
- All DEI training materials (slides, videos, handouts, agendas) from last 3 years
- Training attendance records or sign-in sheets
- Any "cultural competence," "implicit bias," or "privilege" training curricula
- Mandatory trainings or enrollment lists
- Trainer contracts or curriculum vendors

**Submit:**
- PDFs, Word docs, or video links; full curriculum and any assessments

---

### Category 6: Program Eligibility & Participation
- Program eligibility criteria for any housing, services, or benefits programs
- Any "equity plans," "strategic plans," or program descriptions mentioning diversity or underrepresented groups
- Enrollment data or participant lists (summary only, no PII)
- Scholarship or grant program criteria
- Any programs restricted to specific racial, ethnic, or demographic groups

**Submit:**
- Policy documents and summary enrollment/outcome data

---

### Category 7: Facilities & Access Policies
- Policies for restrooms, locker rooms, dormitories, or other intimate spaces
- Any gender-specific or race-specific facility designations or access rules
- Athletic competition policies or rules

**Submit:**
- Policy documents only

---

### Category 8: Third-Party Agreements & Monitoring
- Grants, subcontracts, MOUs (Memoranda of Understanding) with third parties (last 3 years)
- Any nondiscrimination clauses in agreements
- Monitoring reports or corrective action plans for third parties
- Any terminations due to discriminatory practices

**Submit:**
- Executed agreements (redact as needed); monitoring records

---

### Category 9: Complaints & Retaliation Records
- Complaints related to discrimination or DEI practices (last 5 years)
- Investigations and resolutions
- Any documented retaliation or adverse actions against individuals who complained or objected to DEI policies
- Anti-retaliation policies and complaint procedures
- Confidential reporting mechanisms or hotline records (summary only)

**Submit:**
- Complaint logs (no individual names, if preferred); investigation summaries

---

### Category 10: Any Other Relevant Documentation
- Strategic plans, board meeting minutes, or departmental directives mentioning diversity or equity
- Communications (emails, memos) discussing DEI policies or goals
- Metrics or KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) tied to diversity outcomes
- Links to any public websites or online materials describing DEI initiatives

**Submit:**
- Any documents you believe are relevant to this audit

## What Happens Next

1. We will review all submitted documents against the Attorney General guidance.
2. We will score policies and practices on a 0–3 risk scale (0 = compliant, 3 = clear violation).
3. We will produce a findings summary and recommendations for remediation (if needed).
4. We will meet with your team to discuss findings and corrective action plan.
