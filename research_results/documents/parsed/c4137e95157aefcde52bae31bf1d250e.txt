        NOTICE OF RESCISSION: Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964

Please be advised that the Federal Highway Administration (FHWA)          has rescinded the following the
previously issued and outdated documents:

    e    Title VI: Nondiscrimination in the Federal Aid Highway Program Handbook
             o   Publication: FHWA-HCR-07-006

    e   = Title VI: Nondiscrimination in the Federal Aid Highway Program Desk
             o   Reference:     FHWA-HCR-07-0001

    e    Limited English Proficiency Program and the Federal-Aid Highway Program Handbook
             o   Publication: FHWA-HCR-07-0010

    e    Title VI Considerations in the Major Federal-Aid Highway Programs
             o   Unknown Publication Number

    e    Your Rights Under Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, brochure.
             o   Publication: FHWA-HCR-06-003

    e    Preventing Discrimination in the Federal Aid Program: Systematic Interdisciplinary Approach
         Reference Notebook
             o   Unknown      Publication Number

    e    March 22, 2006 Memorandum         on Processing Complaints filed under the Americans with
         Disabilities Act of 1990 and Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964

    e    Impacts of the Civil Rights Restoration Act of 1987 on FHWA programs, September 2, 1992.
             o   Classification Code: N 4720.6, superseded by 49 CFR 21.23, September 2002

    e    All Title VI processes and procedures included in the Procedures Manual for Processing External
         Complaints of Discrimination, Federal Highway Administration, July 9, 2010.

These materials are not in use by FHWA and should not be relied on or referenced by ODOT recipients
and subrecipients. Please be sure to remove these materials from any public-facing websites and ensure
the documents are not provided as part of any training materials or presentations.
