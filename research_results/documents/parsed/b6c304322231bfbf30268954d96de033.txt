What do Public Health
Practitioners Do?
1. Focus on disease prevention
2. Concerned with the health of large populations


   Public Health Practitioners contribute to research,
 teaching, advocacy, evaluation, analysis, leadership,
 and policy development to improve the health of our
        communities, our nation, and our world.
