EXECUTIVE      SUMMARY:     The Clackamas County Sheriff's Office has requested that
Clackamas County Fleet Services purchase twelve (12) 2024 Dodge Pursuit AWD Durangos
from Roberson Motors through the State of Oregon Contract # 1651.            These vehicles will be
assigned to the Sheriffs Office Patrol Division and will replace aging vehicles that are currently
assigned to the patrol division.

PROCUREMENT         PROCESS:        Approval of the purchase is being requested under the Local
Contract Review Board Rule C-046-0400, Authority of Cooperative Procurements. The
purchase will be made using cooperative contract #1651       of the State of Oregon Cooperative
Purchasing Agreement Program from Roberson Motors. A notice of Intent to Purchase the nine
(12) 2024 Dodge Pursuit AWD Durangos was issued on November 28th, 2023. No comments
were received at the time of closing on December 2nd, 2023.

RECOMMENDATION: Staff recommends the Board approve this purchase and authorizes
Procurement staff to issue the PO on behalf of the County.
