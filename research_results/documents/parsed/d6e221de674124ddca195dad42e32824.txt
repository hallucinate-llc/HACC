                                                                                                              SUMMARY
                                 The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
                                 to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
                                 measure as introduced.

                                     Requires school districts to provide instruction on racist history of this country and state. Di-
                                 rects State Board of Education to adopt requirements for instruction.
                                     Directs Department of Education to convene advisory group for purposes of assisting State
                                 Board of Education in adopting requirements for instruction on racist history and recommending
                                 curricula.
                                         Declares       emergency,           effective        on passage.
