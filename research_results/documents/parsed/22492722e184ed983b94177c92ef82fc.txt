                                                                                         SUMMARY
                The following summary is not prepared by the sponsors of the measure and is not a part of the body thereof subject
                to consideration by the Legislative Assembly. It is an editor’s brief statement of the essential features of the
                measure.

                    Establishes COFA Dental Program in Oregon Health Authority to provide dental care to low-
                income citizens of Pacific Islands in Compact of Free Association who reside in Oregon, qualify for
                medical assistance through Oregon Supplemental Income Program and lack access to afforda-
                ble dental coverage. Specifies eligibility requirements for program and duties of authority in ad-
                ministering program.
