    WELCOME TO THE WIC PROGRAM!
    This is your WIC shopping guide. It shows what foods WIC
    approves. WIC provides healthy food benefits every month
    that you are enrolled and eligible for the program. These
    nutritious foods are specifically chosen to promote good
    health, growth, and development. With your eWIC card, you
    can only buy foods that are approved by WIC and in your
    current benefits. Your nutritionist can give you additional tips
    and ways to cook and prepare your WIC foods.

                                                         SHOPPING TIPS
YOUR WIC FOOD BENEFITS
‚ÄĘ WIC food benefits expire every 30 days. Shop
   early during your benefit period to prevent unused
   benefits from expiring. Food benefits can‚Äôt be
   replaced after the expiration date.
‚ÄĘ Up to three months of food benefits may be issued
   at a time. The next benefits are not automatically
   added. Contact your WIC office as directed to
   receive more benefits.
‚ÄĘ Keep your card to receive your next food benefits.
‚ÄĘ Make sure to keep your card safe and report
   damaged, lost, or stolen cards to your WIC office.
‚ÄĘ WIC food benefits can be spent at any Wisconsin
   WIC authorized grocery store. Ask your WIC office
   for a list of local stores.

RESPONSIBILITIES
‚ÄĘ No substitutions, rain checks, or IOUs are allowed.
‚ÄĘ Only purchase the foods and formula you need.
‚ÄĘ Return unused food and formula to your WIC office.
‚ÄĘ WIC foods and formulas cannot be returned or
   exchanged for cash, credit, or other items.
‚ÄĘ WIC foods and formula cannot be offered for sale,
   sold, traded, or given away.

    BEFORE YOU SHOP
    ‚ÄĘ Find your current balance by using the Wisconsin MyWIC
       app, website or phone number on the back of the card,
       WIC Shopping List, or your most recent store receipt.
    ‚ÄĘ Plan which foods to buy and review the information
       in this guide.
    ‚ÄĘ When possible, avoid shopping around midnight. Cash
       register systems often update during this time, preventing
       you from making an eWIC purchase.

    AS YOU SHOP
    ‚ÄĘ To prevent problems at checkout, use this guide, the
       MyWIC app, or your WIC shopping list to help you choose
       the right brands, varieties, and sizes of foods.
    ‚ÄĘ Only the foods in your benefit balance and listed in this
       guide may be purchased with your eWIC card.




    AT THE CHECKOUT
    ‚ÄĘ Tell the cashier you are using your eWIC card and ask if
       you need to separate your groceries.
    ‚ÄĘ Use your eWIC card first, then FoodShare or any other
       forms of payment.
    ‚ÄĘ Some cash register systems print a mid-purchase receipt,
       listing which foods will be deducted from your WIC
       benefits. Carefully review this receipt.
    ‚ÄĘ If there are food items you thought would be covered
       by WIC benefits but are not, ask to have them removed
       if you do not want to buy them with another form of
       payment. Swipe your eWIC card again and
       approve the purchase.

    ‚ÄĘ If you enter your PIN wrong four times, it will lock. To
