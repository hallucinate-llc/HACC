Using ORPIN to find Clackamas County business opportunities
ORPIN — the Oregon Procurement Information Network — is the state’s #1 resource for viewing public agency contract
and business opportunities.

Clackamas County, and most state and local government agencies, use ORPIN to notify vendors of contracting
opportunities, post bid and proposal documents and keep proposers up to date on opportunities and contract awards. To be
notified of opportunities, vendors must register with ORPIN.

To participate, simply register on ORPIN and then log in to search for opportunities that match your business offerings.
Vendors can also sign up for email notifications based on your field of expertise, location and business profile.
