 Annual Contributions Contract (ACC)
 The ACC is the mechanism through which the PHA receives funding, defines the agency’s obliga-
 tions, and outlines remedies for breaches of contract. Should there be a breach of contract, HUD
 will pursue remedial actions. Obligations under the ACC include but are not limited to:
 ¢    Cooperation agreements
 ¢    Operating budgets
 ¢«   Depository Agreements and General Fund
 ¢«   Pooling of funds
 ¢    Books of account, records, and government access
