RE: Homeownership Master Servicer — Request for Proposals, #914-4695-21

  OHCS has issued Addendum #1 to Request for Proposals (“RFP”) #914-4695-21, which
  requests proposals for a master loan servicer to support servicing needs for morigages
  sold. Mortgages may be securitized into To Be Announced (“TBA”) securities into the
  secondary markets or as Mortgage Revenue Bonds (“MRB’s”) (collectively hereinafter
  referred to as the “Program’’). OHCS intends to select a master servicer that will provide
  purchasing, pooling, selling and servicing of mortgage loans, coordinate with the
  administrator of the TBA program, which is currently Hilltop Securities, Inc., and consulting
  on overall design and performance of the Program with OHCS staff for the purpose of
  providing servicing for the mortgage loans.

   Amendment       Purpose
  This Addendum #1 amends the RFP by adding additional exclusions to the proposal
  page limits, updates 4.10.3, Round 1 Evaluation Criteria, |. Proposer’s Price (Total
  Maximum 80 points}, #1 only, and replaces Attachment A, Appendix B. Please see
  Addendum #1 for the full details.
