AIIM Announces Keynote Speakers for the AI+IM Global Summit 2026 November 21, 2025 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE AIIM Announces Keynote Speakers for the AI+IM Global Summit 2026 Industry-leading experts Oliver Patel and Eryn Peters to deliver opening and closing keynotes at premier event for information management professionals SILVER SPRING, MD, USA — The Association for Intelligent Information Management announced he keynote speakers for the AI+IM Global Summit 2026, being held April 28-30 in Baltimore, Maryland, USA. Oliver Patel, Head of Enterprise AI Governance at AstraZeneca, will deliver the opening keynote, while Eryn Peters , future of work evangelist and AI transformation strategist, will close the summit. The two-and-a-half-day event is for information professionals working with unstructured data and preparing it for AI—whether that means leading digital transformation, managing compliance and governance, optimizing content systems, or implementing AI-driven operations. Patel's opening keynote, "Frontier Challenges in Enterprise AI Governance: The Role of Information Management Leaders," addresses the evolving regulatory landscape—from the EU AI Act to ISO 42001—and presents a framework for implementing enterprise AI governance. Drawing on his experience developing governance frameworks for 95,000 employees at AstraZeneca, Patel will examine how AI governance differs from traditional information and data governance and discuss how information management functions can contribute to responsible AI implementation. Peters closes the summit with "The Accountability Advantage: Building Strategic Influence in the AI Era," focusing on how information management professionals can build influence within their organizations during a period of significant change. Attendees will receive the 2030 IM Leader Framework and guidance on initiating conversations with HR, legal, and technical teams. "Information management practitioners are uniquely positioned to ensure accurate, responsible, and trusted data for AI," said Tori Miller Liu, President and CEO of AIIM. "They're already playing a major role in AI governance and implementation. These keynotes will give our attendees both the knowledge framework and the practical playbook they need to lead with confidence." Beyond Traditional Conference Formats The AI+IM Global Summit is designed for active learning through hands-on workshops, live demonstrations, and facilitated peer discussions. Each session encourages participation and collaboration, whether attendees are evaluating AI tools, solving challenges in small groups, or exchanging ideas in interactive panels. Central to the summit experience are Cohorts—small, pre-assigned groups of like-minded peers that support deeper learning and meaningful networking before, during, and after the event. These groups provide a space to exchange ideas, crowdsource solutions, and refine learning goals with others who share similar challenges. Cohorts are assigned on a first-come basis, with early registrants having the best opportunity to be matched with groups aligned to their interests or industry. About the Speakers Oliver Patel is a global authority on enterprise AI governance. He serves as AI Governance Faculty Member at the International Association of Privacy Professionals, where he co-developed the AI Governance Professional certification and has trained thousands of professionals worldwide. He is a member of the OECD Expert Group on AI Risk and Accountability and creator of the Enterprise AI Governance newsletter with over 6,300 subscribers. His forthcoming book, Fundamentals of AI Governance , will serve as the definitive visual guide for implementing enterprise AI governance. Eryn Peters is co-founder of the AI Maturity Index, whose research has impacted over 4.5 million workers across more than 190 countries. A keynote speaker at LEAP Conference and presenter at London Business School and the ISG AI Summit, she is a regular Forbes contributor and editor of the Weekly Workforce newsletter. Peters is a CCMP™ Certified Change Management Professional and has been named ISG's Digital Innovator of the Year and a Top Women Leader in SaaS. Registration and more information can be found at https://www.aiim.org/aiim26 . Early bird pricing ends on December 31, 2025. About the Association for Intelligent Information Management Founded in 1944, the Association for Intelligent Information Management (AIIM) is a nonprofit organization serving information leaders in over 67 countries worldwide. AIIM's vision is to create a world where every organization benefits from intelligent information and data management to achieve better business outcomes. AIIM helps information leaders manage and prepare unstructured data for AI and automation by providing advice, certification, training, and peer-to-peer support. Through practical and approachable resources, AIIM enables organizations to leverage their information assets effectively, ultimately leading to improved business performance and success.
