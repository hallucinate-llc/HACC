                            No-Cost, Online DEI Resources and Trainings
ABOUT THESE RESOURCES | The following resources are curated by Oregon Youth Authority’s
Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (or DEI) team. While much of this material aligns with our approach
and core practices, we are not responsible for the current content or any changes that may occur.
Access these resources to learn how systemic racism and inequities are embedded in systems
throughout history and especially in the justice system. Grounding ourselves in these learnings helps
us better understand the importance of infusing equity into the fabric of all we do. Explore these at
your own pace, thoughtfully, and with a desire for continuous growth and learning.
