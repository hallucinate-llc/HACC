KEY FINDINGS BY CATEGORY
================================================================================
[A] LIKELY BINDING - OREGON STATUTES (ORS)
-------------------------------------------
These are state laws that almost certainly apply to HACC:
• ORS 456 - Housing Authorities Act
→ Establishes powers, duties, and obligations of housing authorities
→ Contains non-discrimination and equal opportunity provisions
→ BINDING as enabling legislation for HACC
• ORS 659A - Discrimination
→ Prohibits discrimination in employment, housing, public accommodations
→ Defines protected classes (race, sex, religion, disability, etc.)
→ Enforced by Oregon Bureau of Labor & Industries (BOLI)
→ BINDING on all employers and housing providers in Oregon
• ORS 279A/279C - Public Contracting
→ Governs how public agencies (including housing authorities) contract
→ Contains MWESB provisions (Minority/Women/Emerging Small Business)
→ Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) requirements
→ Good faith effort standards for outreach to certified firms
→ BINDING on public contracting activities
[B] LIKELY BINDING - OREGON ADMINISTRATIVE RULES (OAR)
-------------------------------------------------------
• OAR Chapter 813 - Housing & Community Services rules
→ Implements ORS 456 and related housing statutes
→ 11 DEI term occurrences found
→ BINDING as implementing regulations
• OAR Chapter 137 - Public Contracting rules
→ Implements ORS 279A/279C
→ Details MWESB certification and compliance procedures
→ BINDING on public contracting
[C] FEDERAL REQUIREMENTS
------------------------
• Fair Housing Act (through HUD)
→ Applies to housing providers receiving federal funding
→ Prohibits discrimination in housing
→ BINDING if HACC receives HUD funds
• Section 3 (if applicable)
→ Economic opportunities for low-income persons
→ Applies to certain HUD-funded projects
→ BINDING if threshold funding amounts are met
[D] ADVISORY/GUIDANCE (Review for Relevance)

• Governor's Executive Orders - May apply to state agencies only
• OHCS guidance - Best practices, may not be legally binding

WHAT THIS MEANS FOR HACC
================================================================================
EMPLOYMENT DECISIONS:
→ Must comply with ORS 659A anti-discrimination provisions
→ Cannot discriminate based on protected classes
→ Must provide equal employment opportunity
CONTRACTING/PROCUREMENT:
→ Must follow ORS 279A/279C public contracting procedures
→ Must make good faith efforts to utilize MWESB certified firms
→ Must document outreach and bidder participation
HOUSING/LEASING:
→ Must comply with Fair Housing Act (federal)
→ Must comply with ORS 659A housing discrimination prohibitions
→ Cannot discriminate in tenant selection or lease terms
BUSINESS DECISIONS:
→ ORS 456 governs general powers and duties of housing authorities
→ Must follow administrative procedures (ORS 183) for rule-making
→ Subject to public records and meetings laws
