Related Northwest

Related Northwest delivers high-quality, affordable multifamily communities

In 2018, Related's affordable housing footprint expanded into the Pacific Northwest to address the urgent regional housing needs and develop communities with housing and services.

Our Portfolio

Beacon at Glisan Landing

Portland

Located in the Montavilla neighborhood of Portland, on a site originally purchased by Metro, Glisan Landing is 137 units of affordable family and permanent supportive housing across two buildings. A public-private partnership with Related Northwest, Immigrant and Refugee Community Organization (IRCO), Metro and Catholic Charities of Oregon, Glisan Landing provides affordable and supportive housing for low-income households with a range of incomes from 30% to 60% Area Median Income (AMI) with onsite amenities, resident services and employment opportunities.

View This Property

Aldea at Glisan Landing

Portland

Located in the Montavilla neighborhood of Portland, on a 1.65-acre site originally purchased by Metro, Glisan Landing is 137 units of affordable family and permanent supportive housing across two buildings. A public-private partnership with Related Northwest, Immigrant and Refugee Community Organization (IRCO), Metro and Catholic Charities of Oregon, Glisan Landing provides affordable and supportive housing for low-income households with a range of incomes from 30% to 60% Area Median Income (AMI) with onsite amenities, resident services and employment opportunities.

View This Property

Terrace Glen Apartments

Tigard
Located on a 2.88-acre parcel in the heart of the Metzger neighborhood in the Washington Square District, Terrace Glen Apartments is an affordable, intergenerational development with 144 apartments for low and very low-income families and seniors.

View This Property

Cathedral Village Apartments

Portland
Located on 1.2 acres in the historic North Portland neighborhood of St. Johns, Cathedral Village Apartments is a 4-story affordable, permanent supportive housing development that provides equitable access to families experiencing houselessness or at risk of houselessness.

View This Property

Crescent Court Apartments

Portland
Located on a 2-acre site in Outer Southeast Portland, Crescent Court Apartments is a development of 138 units of low-income housing for families and households experiencing or at risk of houselessness.

View This Property

Cedar Commons

Portland
Located in the Outer Southeast Portland area within the Powellhurst-Gilbert District on 1.07 acres, Cedar Commons is a 60-unit affordable and supportive housing community for low-income individuals.

View This Property

Alder 9

Portland

In partnership with Centro Cultural, Alder 9 will be a 159-unit community for low-income seniors and intergenerational families at 30% and 60% Area Median Income.  Located in the heart of Central Eastside Portland, twenty-five units will be set aside for Permanent Supportive Housing to serve those who are houseless or at-risk of houselessness. 

View This Property

Hillside Park

Milwaukie

Related Northwest, in partnership with the Housing Authority of Clackamas County (HACC), will redevelop a 7.5-acre portion of Hillside Park, an existing 13.7-acre public housing project constructed in the 1940s. The aging site is being redeveloped and expanded into a walkable, amenity-rich community. 

View This Property

Pacifica

Seaside

Located on Oregon’s coast in the town of Seaside on a two-acre parcel of land donated by Clatsop County, Pacifca is a visionary effort to transform surplus public land into housing with integrated social services.  A public-private partnership between Related Northwest, Centro Cultural and the City of Seaside, Pacifica will be a 69-unit affordable workforce housing development for households with incomes at 60% Area Median Income (AMI). Pacifica will consist of three, three-story walk-up buildings with studios, one, two, and three-bedroom apartments.
