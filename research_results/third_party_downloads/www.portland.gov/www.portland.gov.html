Administrative and Council Leadership

  • Keith Wilson

    Mayor

    Keith Wilson is the 54th Mayor of Portland, OR. He was elected in November 2024 and took office in January 2025.

  • Simone Rede

    City Auditor

    Simone Rede is the City Auditor of Portland, OR. She was first elected in May 2022 and was re-elected in November 2024.

  • Michael Jordan

    City Administrator

    Michael Jordan is serving as the City Administrator of Portland, OR. He was appointed by Mayor Ted Wheeler in May 2024.

  • Elana Pirtle-Guiney

    District 2 - Council President

    Elana Pirtle-Guiney is the City Council President. She was voted in as council president in January 2025.

  • Tiffany Koyama Lane

    District 3 - Council Vice President

    Tiffany Koyama Lane is the City Council Vice President. She was voted in as council vice president in January 2025.
