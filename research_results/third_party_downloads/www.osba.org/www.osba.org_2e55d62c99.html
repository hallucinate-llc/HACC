Help! Our Superintendent is Leaving! Now What?

Thursday, Dec. 18, 2025 12-1 p.m.

Hiring an executive is one of the most important school board responsibilities, and it can feel overwhelming for board members and administrative professionals. Reprising a popular session from the OSBA Annual Convention, we will start with a short overview of the executive search process from OSBA’s perspective, including how OSBA compares with what other consultants provide. A panel of consultants who have navigated searches will share lessons learned, challenges and strategies for success. You’ll gain a clearer sense of what to expect and how to plan.

Board Goal Setting for Student Success

Thursday, Jan. 15, 2026 12-1 p.m.

Research shows that board behaviors and characteristics directly affect student outcomes. Spring is an ideal time to take stock — celebrate what’s strong, identify what needs tuning and set clear goals for growth. In this webinar, we’ll walk you through the Balanced Governance Board Self-Assessment and show how it can guide your board’s next steps in development.
