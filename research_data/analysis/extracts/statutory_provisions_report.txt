CRITICAL ANALYSIS NOTES

ORS Chapter 456 - Housing Authorities:
  • Establishes powers and duties of housing authorities in Oregon
  • May contain non-discrimination and equal opportunity requirements

ORS Chapter 659A - Discrimination:
  • Oregon's primary anti-discrimination statute
  • Applies to employment, housing, and public accommodations
  • Enforced by Oregon Bureau of Labor and Industries (BOLI)

ORS Chapter 279A/279C - Public Contracting:
  • Governs public agency contracting (including housing authorities)
  • May include MWESB (Minority/Women/Emerging Small Business) requirements
  • Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) provisions
