## Key Indicators to Look For

### ✅ Signs of Binding Obligation:
- "shall", "must", "required", "mandatory"
- "all agencies", "statewide", "local government"
- "condition of funding", "federal requirement"
- "policy", "procedure", "standard"
- "contract requirement", "grant agreement"

### ✅ Signs of DEI Implementation:
- Explicit diversity targets or quotas
- Preferential treatment language
- Proxy criteria (zip code, first-generation, disadvantaged)
- Segregated programs or track records
- Diversity committee requirements
- Mandatory training
- Supplier diversity programs

### ⚠️ Proxies to Watch (Bondi memo concern):
- "underserved", "underrepresented"
- "disadvantaged", "low-income"
- "BIPOC", "communities of color"
- "lived experience"
- "cultural competence"
- "safe space", "inclusive"
- "environmental justice"
