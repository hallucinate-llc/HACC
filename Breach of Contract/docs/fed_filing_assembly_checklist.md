- HACC's own records triggered the relocation process.
- HACC's own records show relocation remained incomplete when eviction was pursued.
- HACC's own records tie Blossom/Hillside Manor to replacement housing.

## Assertions To Phrase Carefully

- Any statement that Quantum expressly lied about receipt:
  - plead on information and belief unless independently corroborated.
- Any direct HUD liability theory:
  - keep secondary unless notice-and-response records are in hand.
