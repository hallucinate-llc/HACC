# Finalization Worksheet Before Filing

Use this as a last-pass checklist after drafting and before e-filing.

## Case Identity

1. County: [COUNTY]
2. Court division: [CIRCUIT / HOUSING / FED TRACK]
3. Case number: [CASE NUMBER]
4. Plaintiff legal name exactly as docketed.
5. Defendant names exactly as docketed.

## Third-Party Identity

1. Exact legal name of Quantum Residential entity.
2. Registered agent name and service address.
3. Any DBA/affiliate names to include in pleading.

## Signature Blocks

1. Filing party name(s) and role (pro se or counsel).
2. Mailing address.
3. Phone and email.
4. Signature lines and dates completed in all documents.

## Procedural Rule Verification

1. ORCP 29 applicability confirmed.
2. ORCP 30 applicability confirmed.
3. ORCP 33 C timing and leave requirements confirmed.
4. Local county supplementary rules checked.
5. Hearing reservation and response timeline verified.

## Service Requirements

1. Current parties service method confirmed.
2. Third-party service method confirmed.
3. Certificate of service completed with exact recipients and method.
4. Proof of service retention plan (mail receipt/efile confirmation/process server return).

## Exhibit Finalization

1. Exhibits labeled A through M consistently across all docs.
2. Exhibit titles match label table exactly.
3. Exhibit references in declaration and motion are consistent.
4. Duplicate files removed from filing packet.
5. Optional sensitive data redactions reviewed.
6. Exhibit M (HACC phase2 2024.pdf \u2014 Hillside Park Apartments Phase II General Information Notice, September 19, 2024) confirmed in binder as the foundational Section 18 / TPV relocation trigger document.
7. If using the Hillside Park public webpage, decide whether it will be cited only in argument, attached as a supplemental public-source note, or presented through a judicial-notice request under local practice.
