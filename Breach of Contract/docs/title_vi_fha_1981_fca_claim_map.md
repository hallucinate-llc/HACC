## Cherry Picking vs. Lemon Dropping Use

These are best used as descriptive theories, not standalone legal counts.

The current record most strongly supports a `lemon dropping` description:

- application accepted or received,
- then not processed,
- not transmitted,
- not lawfully resolved,
- while the household remained exposed to delay and eviction.

If discovery later shows that other households were affirmatively moved forward during the same period, then `cherry picking` becomes the matching comparison theory.
