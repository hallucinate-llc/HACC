# Parkside Integration Notes For FED Drafts

Use these points when customizing the FED papers.

## Best Parkside Framing

1. HACC affirmatively identified Parkside Heights as an available relocation option on March 26, 2026.
2. The Parkside path was not hypothetical; HACC described specific next steps.
3. The household then completed and submitted the Parkside Heights application.
4. Parkside Heights acknowledged receipt of that application on March 30, 2026.
5. The reason the Parkside path was not complete on the present record was not simply household inaction; Parkside stated that the two-bedroom units were still under construction with estimated occupancy not until the end of April.

## Best Legal Uses

- substantial compliance
- prevention / hindrance
- promissory estoppel / reliance
- rebuttal to any theory that defendants refused relocation or failed to pursue options

## Careful Limit

Do not overstate Parkside as a fully executed lease unless additional documents are found showing:

- approval
- assigned specific unit
- signed lease
- accepted hold deposit
- HACC tenancy approval / RTA approval
