# Section 18 Targeted RFP and Subpoena Requests

Purpose: Obtain records needed to prove or disprove statutory compliance under 42 U.S.C. 1437p and 24 CFR part 970.

## A. Requests for Production to PHA / Housing Authority

1. Produce all HUD approval letters or determinations for the subject development under Section 18.
2. Produce the complete Section 18 application packet submitted to HUD.
3. Produce all attachments/exhibits submitted with that application.
4. Produce all drafts and revisions of the Section 18 application.
5. Produce all communications with HUD regarding application sufficiency or corrections.
6. Produce the relocation plan submitted or maintained for the subject development.
7. Produce all relocation timetables by household and unit.
8. Produce all notices sent to this household related to demolition/disposition or relocation.
9. Produce proof of service/delivery for each notice sent to this household.
10. Produce all records identifying the displacement date used for this household.
11. Produce all records showing determination of comparable replacement housing for this household.
12. Produce all records showing HQS status of proposed replacement units.
13. Produce all records comparing neighborhood desirability factors used in replacement offers.
14. Produce all records of relocation expense estimates for this household.
15. Produce all records of relocation expense payments or denials for this household.
16. Produce all records of counseling or advisory services offered to this household.
17. Produce all disability accommodation requests from this household.
18. Produce all determinations/responses to those accommodation requests.
19. Produce all records of consultation with affected residents for this action.
20. Produce all resident comments received and agency evaluations/responses.
21. Produce all records of consultation with resident councils/RAB.
22. Produce all records of consultation with local government officials.
23. Produce the board resolution approving this specific demolition/disposition application and date of execution.
24. Produce the PHA Annual Plan language describing the same action and timetable.
25. Produce all environmental review approvals and supporting records under 24 CFR part 58 or part 50.
26. Produce all civil-rights compliance certifications tied to this application.
27. If disposition occurred or is planned, produce all appraisals and fair-market-value analyses.
28. If disposition occurred or is planned, produce settlement/closing statements and proceeds ledgers.
29. Produce records showing use of net proceeds and any HUD waiver related to debt-retirement priority.
30. Produce all policies and guidance relied on to decide that displacement could proceed for this household.

## B. Subpoena Targets and Key Documents

1. HUD Field Office
- Approval letter, deficiency notices, condition letters, and correspondence log for the Hillside Park Phase II Section 18 application.

2. Local Government (Chief Executive Office)
- Consultation letters, response letters, meeting notes.

3. Appraiser / Valuation Firm (if disposition)
- Appraisal engagement, final appraisal, assumptions and comps.

4. Quantum Residential (Property Manager — confirmed in HACC Administrative Plan as property management company for a PHA-owned HAP contract development in Clackamas County)
- All management agreements, owner-participation agreements, or operating agreements between Quantum Residential and HACC or any HACC-related entity for any property in Clackamas County.
- All communications between HACC and Quantum Residential regarding the Blossom & Community Apartments intake, waitlist, and application process.
- All intake packets received by Quantum Residential from or on behalf of the Barber/Cortez household.
- All internal communications regarding that intake packet, its receipt, its transmission, or its non-transmission to HACC.
- All unit-availability, accessibility, and leasing records for Blossom & Community Apartments for the period October 2024 through March 2026.
- Any HAP contract or owner-participation agreement between Quantum Residential or any related entity and HACC covering Blossom & Community Apartments.
- Any correspondence or records concerning Quantum Residential's role in the Hillside Park redevelopment relocation program, including Tenant Protection Voucher processing.

5. HACC / Housing Authority of Clackamas County (supplemental to RFP)
- The complete HACC Administrative Plan entries for Blossom & Community Apartments, Hillside Manor Apartments, and Clayton Mohr Commons, including: PBV waitlist policies, excluded-unit classifications, PHA-owned unit inspection and management arrangements, and all HAP contracts.
- All management or owner-participation agreements in HACC's possession covering Blossom & Community Apartments.
- The full Section 18 application submitted to HUD for Hillside Park, including the relocation plan, consultation records, and board resolution.

## C. Authentication-Ready Metadata to Demand

1. Creation date, author, custodian, and system of record for each produced item.
2. Native files with metadata where available.
3. Mail or delivery logs for all notices.
4. Communication exports with thread IDs and timestamps.

## D. Enforcement Hooks

If production is incomplete, move to compel using the direct statutory/regulatory duty linked to the missing category (notice, relocation, consultation, HUD approval, or proceeds handling).
