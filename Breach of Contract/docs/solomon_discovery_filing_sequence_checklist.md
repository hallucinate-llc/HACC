## 1. Fill In Before Service

1. Insert the Clackamas County case number in both captioned drafts.
2. Confirm the exact service name and address for HACC or HACC's counsel.
3. Fill in the certificate-of-service date and service method in the HACC discovery draft.
4. Identify the likely subpoena custodians:
   - phone-system custodian
   - visitor / building-access custodian
   - CRM / case-management custodian
5. Check whether the court requires a separate subpoena form, notice, witness fee, or advance notice to the opposing party before third-party service.

## 2. Best First Service

Serve first:

1. [solomon_interference_hacc_discovery_captioned_draft.md](/home/barberb/HACC/Breach%20of%20Contract/docs/solomon_interference_hacc_discovery_captioned_draft.md)

Why first:

- HACC may produce the key answer without third-party motion practice.
- HACC may identify the actual custodian names for the later subpoenas.
- HACC may admit there is no written request from Benjamin, Jane, or Julio.

## 3. What To Demand First From HACC

The most important items to watch for in HACC's response are:

1. any record naming who requested Benjamin's January 2026 lease removal
2. the author and metadata for the January 1, 2026 lease amendment
3. any note or call log mentioning Solomon
4. any explanation of `restored the restrained party to the lease`
5. any claim that Benjamin, Jane, or Julio requested the change

## 4. Decision Point After HACC Responds

If HACC produces:

- a named source for the lease removal:
  - compare that source against the Solomon theory and update the pleadings if needed
- no lawful household request:
  - use that as affirmative support that the lease was changed without valid authorization
- incomplete logs or metadata:
  - move to the subpoena stage immediately

## 5. Best Subpoena Order

If HACC's response is incomplete, issue subpoenas in this order:

1. CRM / case-management custodian
2. phone-system custodian
3. visitor / building-access custodian
4. optional county administrative or counsel-routing custodian

Why this order:

- CRM logs are most likely to identify the user, source note, and approval trail for the lease change
- phone logs are next best for proving off-record contact
- visitor logs are useful, but often weaker or less complete than system logs
