## Final Assembly Note

Before filing, align the exhibit lettering in the FED packet with the lettering the court will actually see. If you reuse the federal exhibit lettering (`O`, `P`, etc.), keep it consistent across the answer, declaration, and any joinder motion. If you renumber for the state FED packet, update the draft citations before filing.
