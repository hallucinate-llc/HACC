# Hearing Emphasis Priority Sheet

This sheet is designed to keep the live argument disciplined by separating:

1. what to lead with;
2. what to use carefully;
3. what to hold in reserve; and
4. the shortest framing for housing and probate hearings.

## Lead With

### Housing

1. `HACC triggered Section 18 relocation duties.`
   - strongest law-first branch
   - do not overstate it as a flat anti-eviction statute
   - strongest formulation: HACC should not obtain possession while the triggered relocation process remained materially incomplete

2. `HACC's own papers kept the household inside an unresolved relocation path.`
   - January 8, 2026 Blossom-or-TPV direction
   - March 26, 2026 three-option / April 30 framework
   - use this because it is HACC's own written administration record

3. `HACC admitted a packet-routing failure in the same path.`
   - strongest causation point
   - this is one of the cleanest reasons the case should not be treated as ordinary tenant default

4. `The household kept participating.`
   - submitted applications
   - followed up on voucher / portability
   - submitted Parkside after the March 19 voucher marker
   - use this to defeat the "tenant-caused noncompletion" narrative

### Probate

5. `Coercive writ-based transport/removal needs a clean predicate order and notice basis.`
   - strongest probate-side procedural point

6. `Plenary / placement / fee relief is overbroad on this disputed record.`
   - keep this tied to Chapter 125 tailoring

7. `Trust / authority papers were referenced but not produced.`
   - strongest use is to demand production and resist broad control

## Use Carefully

1. `Quantum duty theory`
   - use Quantum as the project-side intake / handling / non-obstruction actor
   - do not push full HACC-equivalent duty unless the agreements are produced

2. `VAWA / safety-transfer theory`
   - use as lawful-administration, confidentiality, and safety-context support
   - do not overstate as a guaranteed completed out-of-county move by a fixed deadline

3. `PBV/RAD hearing-before-eviction point`
   - good reinforcement
   - not the main pillar unless project-role documents get stronger

## Hold In Reserve

1. `Race comparator theory`
   - use only if the other side opens the door or if the judge asks directly

2. `Broad Quantum contract-duty expansion`
   - wait for management agreement / HAP / ground-lease records

3. `True collateral estoppel`
   - current strongest use is narrowing / fencing off issues, not full preclusion victory

4. `Exact invalidity of trust / authority papers`
   - strong reason to demand production
   - weaker as a proved merits point until the actual papers are produced

## Short Framing

### Housing

`HACC triggered relocation duties, directed this household into specific administered paths, admitted a packet-routing failure, kept the paths unresolved, and should not get possession as if this were ordinary tenant default.`

### Probate

`The Court should deny coercive and plenary relief on a disputed record, require a clean writ predicate and notice basis, and refuse to let unproduced trust/authority papers or collateral housing disputes drive sweeping control orders.`
