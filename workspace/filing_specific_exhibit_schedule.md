## Anti-Overstatement Note

The cleanest filing practice is:

1. use `AA` for the March 26 three-path deadline email;
2. use `Y` for the Blossom PBV waitlist / priority-group representation;
3. use `Z` for the Administrative Plan / Hillside Manor / Quantum connection; and
4. use `T` for the February 2 direct email to `blossom@quantumres.com`;
5. use `R` only for the later complaint chain to `charity@magikcorp.com`; and
6. avoid citing `M` for those points when `AA`, `Y`, or `Z` are cleaner and more direct.
