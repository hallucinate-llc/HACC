## Best Filing Combination

If you want the cleanest court packet, I would use:

- `Exhibit T`
- `Exhibit U`
- `Exhibit V`
- keep existing `Exhibit S`
- cite the AFH / Metro / OHCS materials in text or footnotes instead of filing `Exhibit W` or `Exhibit X` as standalone exhibits unless needed

That approach keeps the packet tighter:

- `Exhibit S` already covers the preserved SHS screenshot
- `Exhibit T` gives the direct comparator email
- `Exhibit U` corroborates immediate follow-up and notice
- `Exhibit V` gives the packet-nontransmission proof
