# Declaration Of Benjamin Jay Barber Regarding March 23, 2026 Inspection

IN THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT
FOR THE DISTRICT OF OREGON

Benjamin Jay Barber, Jane Kay Cortez, and Julio Regal Florez-Cortez, Plaintiffs,

v.

Housing Authority of Clackamas County and Quantum Residential, Defendants.

I, Benjamin Jay Barber, declare as follows:

1. I am a plaintiff in this action. I make this declaration from personal knowledge, except where I expressly identify a matter as based on my review of records.
2. My mother, Jane Kay Cortez, and I were present during the HUD NSPIRE inspection at Hillside Park on March 23, 2026.
3. The inspection date is consistent with the written notice dated March 17, 2026, stating that HUD would conduct a property inspection at Hillside Park on Monday, March 23, 2026, between 8:00 AM and 5:00 PM. Exhibit 1.
4. During that inspection, my mother and I heard Charley Skee make statements in substance explaining why we had not yet been moved.
5. In substance, we understood Charley Skee to say that I had been taken off the lease because my brother and my stepfather had been emailing documents to HACC, and that the situation continued until staff were able to sort through the documents after I sent a large batch of materials.
6. I offer paragraph 5 as a statement of what my mother and I heard during the March 23, 2026 inspection, not as a claim that I currently possess a separate written memorialization by Charley Skee using those exact words.
7. My understanding from the records is that Charley Skee was already involved in the JC Household email chain by December 2, 2025, when I sent an email to Ashley Ferron and copied Charley Skee, HACC Public Housing Management, and HACC Relocation. Exhibit 2.
8. My understanding from the records is that HACC later received repeated batches of documentation from me between February 25, 2026 and March 9, 2026 in response to HACC's document demands. Exhibits 6 through 15.
9. My understanding from the records is that I had sought a restraining order against Ashley Ferron by early December 2025, and that protective-order filings against Ashley Ferron existed by January 2026. Exhibits 2 through 5.
10. Based on what my mother and I heard during the March 23, 2026 inspection, and based on the surrounding document history, I understood Charley Skee's statement to mean that HACC had been acting on outside document flows concerning me and the household and that HACC staff associated the delay or non-move with sorting through those materials.

I declare under penalty of perjury under the laws of the United States that the foregoing is true and correct.
