TO DEFENDANTS HOUSING AUTHORITY OF CLACKAMAS COUNTY, QUANTUM RESIDENTIAL, AND THEIR COUNSEL OF RECORD, IF ANY:

PLEASE TAKE NOTICE that Plaintiffs Benjamin Barber, Jane Cortez, and Julio Cortez will move this Court for a temporary restraining order and preliminary injunction under Federal Rule of Civil Procedure 65.

Plaintiffs request emergency relief to preserve the status quo, prevent eviction or displacement, preserve voucher and relocation rights, and require Defendant Housing Authority of Clackamas County to provide Julio Cortez a meaningful and accessible hearing process that does not depend on a written request.

This motion is based on:

1. the complaint;
2. Plaintiffs' Emergency Motion for Temporary Restraining Order and Preliminary Injunction;
3. the Declaration of Benjamin Barber in Support of Emergency Motion;
4. any supplemental witness declaration filed in support of Julio Cortez's hearing-access allegations;
5. the exhibits attached to the emergency motion papers; and
6. such further argument or evidence as the Court may permit.

Plaintiffs request that the Court set the motion for hearing on the earliest available emergency calendar, or grant such temporary relief as is appropriate pending a hearing.
