# Declaration Of Julio Regal Florez-Cortez Regarding Hearing Access

IN THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT
FOR THE DISTRICT OF OREGON

Benjamin Jay Barber, Jane Kay Cortez, and Julio Regal Florez-Cortez, Plaintiffs,

v.

Housing Authority of Clackamas County and Quantum Residential, Defendants.

Civil Action No. ________________

I, Julio Regal Florez-Cortez, declare as follows:

1. I am a plaintiff in this action and make this declaration from personal knowledge.
2. I am part of the household with Benjamin Jay Barber and Jane Kay Cortez that was affected by the HACC notices, lease actions, relocation problems, and hearing-access issues described in the complaint.
3. On or about February 13, 2026, HACC issued a 30-Day Notice of Termination addressed directly to me and stating an effective date of March 19, 2026. Exhibit N.
4. I cannot write in any language.
5. About one week after I received the February 13, 2026 termination notice, I called Ashley Ferron and asked for a hearing orally.
6. I was told that I needed to request a hearing in writing.
7. Because I cannot write, that was not an accessible way for me to ask for a hearing.
8. HACC did not give me a hearing after my oral request.
9. HACC did not provide me any oral, assisted, interpreter-supported, or otherwise accessible method to request or obtain a hearing.
10. I understood that HACC was proceeding against me even though I had asked for a hearing and could not comply with a writing requirement.
11. Without effective relief, I fear losing housing and being denied any meaningful chance to be heard before that happens.
12. I ask that any required hearing process be made accessible and not depend on a written request that I cannot make.

I declare under penalty of perjury under the laws of the United States that the foregoing is true and correct.
