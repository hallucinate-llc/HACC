I, Jane Kay Cortez, declare as follows:

1. I am a plaintiff in this action. I make this declaration from personal knowledge. If called as a witness, I could and would testify competently to the matters stated here.

3. I have physical and cognitive limitations that affect my mobility, my ability to walk, my ability to understand decisions, and my ability to retain memories.
4. Because of those limitations, I need stable and accessible housing conditions.
5. I rely on Benjamin Barber as a live-in caregiver for help with day-to-day functioning, decision-making, and housing-related matters.
6. A one-bedroom arrangement does not meet the household's needs because it does not provide adequate space for caregiving, privacy, mobility-related needs, and the other disability-related needs described in the complaint.

8. I fear losing housing stability if Defendants are allowed to proceed before the Court can require a lawful accommodation and relocation process.
9. I ask the Court to preserve the status quo and require Defendants to provide lawful housing, accommodation, and relocation procedures while this case is pending.
