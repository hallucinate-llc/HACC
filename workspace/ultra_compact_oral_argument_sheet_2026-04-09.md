# Ultra-Compact Oral Argument Sheet

Use this if the Court gives very limited time.

## Housing Version

`Your Honor, this should not be treated as an ordinary holdover. HACC triggered Section 18 relocation duties, directed this household into specific relocation paths, later acknowledged that a packet submitted through the Hillside Manor / Quantum route had not been forwarded to HACC, and as late as March 26 was still describing three unresolved relocation options. On this record, the narrow request is not a merits ruling on every housing issue. It is to deny or withhold possession as premature, or at minimum preserve the housing, voucher, transfer, and application paths while the unresolved relocation process is sorted out.`

## Probate Version

`Your Honor, the petition seeks sweeping coercive and plenary relief on a disputed record. The strongest present points are that the writ-based transport sequence needs a clean predicate order and notice basis, the petition seeks overbroad placement and plenary authority, and trust or authority papers were referenced without being produced. The narrow request is to deny coercive and plenary relief on this record, require a clean predicate basis for any writ-style relief, and fence off collateral housing merits except to the extent strictly necessary.`

## Combined Version

`Your Honor, both branches of this dispute are being overstated by the other side. On the housing side, HACC kept this household inside an unresolved relocation process and should not get possession as if this were ordinary default. On the probate side, Solomon seeks sweeping control and writ-based coercive relief without a clean predicate order, notice basis, or produced authority papers. The narrow and safe course is to preserve the status quo, deny overbroad relief, and require a cleaner record before any coercive step is allowed.`

## Last-Line Ask

`The immediate request is preservation and narrowing, not overbroad final relief.`
