## 4. Wording That Is Safe

Prefer:

- `the record supports`
- `HACC's own documents state`
- `HACC later acknowledged in writing`
- `on the present record`
- `the formal model confirms the normative consequence of the source-linked facts`

## 5. Filing Formulations To Use Instead

Use these formulations in place of broader shorthand:

- Instead of `the graph proves the facts`, use `the formal model confirms the normative consequence of the source-linked facts`.
- Instead of `the contracts say`, cite the actual source: `HACC's March 26, 2026 options email states`, `HACC's January 8, 2026 notice states`, or `HACC's Administrative Plan states`.
- Instead of `HACC caused 100% of the delay`, use `on the present record, a substantial amount, and likely most, of the documented delay was caused by HACC's own administration`.
- Instead of `Quantum definitely violated every federal duty`, use `HACC's own records place Quantum inside the disputed intake-and-relocation chain`.
- Instead of `this conclusively proves race discrimination`, use `the record supports evidence of race-discriminatory obstruction in the same housing path`.

Before filing or arguing:
