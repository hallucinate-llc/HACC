## Filing Note

Defendants submit this coordinated packet in response to Plaintiff's eviction motion.

The packet is organized to address four connected issues arising from the same housing and relocation record:

1. Plaintiff's request for possession should be denied, or at minimum stayed or abated, because the relocation, voucher, transfer, and accommodation path was still incomplete.
2. Narrow interim relief is needed to preserve the housing path and prevent the dispute from becoming moot through loss of housing before adjudication.
3. Quantum Residential Property Management, L.P. should be joined because HACC's own records place Quantum inside the disputed intake-and-relocation chain.
4. Upon joinder or other appearance, Quantum should be required to show cause why the Barber-Cortez Blossom / Hillside Manor application materials were not processed, transmitted, or brought to a clear written result.

This packet is intended to help the Court address the related issues together rather than through fragmented motion practice over the same record.

## Narrow Relief Requested

Defendants respectfully request that the Court:

1. deny possession as premature, or alternatively stay or abate possession;
2. preserve the relocation, voucher, transfer, accommodation, and application path while this case is pending;
3. join Quantum Residential Property Management, L.P.; and
4. require Quantum, upon joinder or appearance, to explain what happened to the Barber-Cortez application materials and to preserve the related records.
