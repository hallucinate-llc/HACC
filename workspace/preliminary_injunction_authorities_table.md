# Preliminary Injunction Authorities Table

This table collects the main authorities used in the joint preliminary-injunction packet and groups them by function.

## Injunction Standard

1. [Winter v. NRDC, 555 U.S. 7 (2008)](https://www.supremecourt.gov/opinions/07pdf/07-1239.pdf)
   Use: four-part federal preliminary-injunction standard.
2. [Alliance for the Wild Rockies v. Cottrell, 632 F.3d 1127 (9th Cir. 2011)](https://cdn.ca9.uscourts.gov/datastore/opinions/2011/02/25/09-35756.pdf)
   Use: Ninth Circuit serious-questions formulation.
3. [Marlyn Nutraceuticals, Inc. v. Mucos Pharma GmbH, 571 F.3d 873 (9th Cir. 2009)](https://cdn.ca9.uscourts.gov/datastore/opinions/2009/07/17/08-55331.pdf)
   Use: mandatory preliminary injunctions are disfavored and must be narrowly framed.

## Joint FHA Duties Against HACC And Quantum

4. [42 U.S.C. § 3617](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?edition=prelim&num=0&req=granuleid%3AUSC-prelim-title42-section3617)
   Use: no coercion, intimidation, threats, or interference with protected fair-housing activity.
5. [24 C.F.R. § 100.400](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-24/subtitle-B/chapter-I/subchapter-A/part-100/subpart-D/section-100.400)
   Use: regulatory implementation of FHA interference / retaliation.
6. [42 U.S.C. § 3604(f)(2)](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?req=granuleid:USC-prelim-title42-section3604&num=0&edition=prelim)
   Use: disability discrimination in terms, conditions, privileges, or services.
7. [42 U.S.C. § 3604(f)(3)(B)](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?req=granuleid:USC-prelim-title42-section3604&num=0&edition=prelim)
   Use: reasonable-accommodation duty.
8. [24 C.F.R. § 100.204](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-24/subtitle-B/chapter-I/subchapter-A/part-100/subpart-B/section-100.204)
   Use: regulatory implementation of FHA reasonable accommodation.

## Section 18 / Relocation Duties

9. [42 U.S.C. § 1437p](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?req=granuleid:USC-prelim-title42-section1437p&num=0&edition=prelim)
   Use: demolition / disposition framework, notice, relocation, comparable housing.
10. [24 C.F.R. § 970.21](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-24/subtitle-B/chapter-IX/part-970/section-970.21)
   Use: relocation requirements, comparable housing, moving expenses, completion of relocation.
11. [HUD Section 18 Overview](https://www.hud.gov/sites/dfiles/PIH/documents/Section%2018%20Overview%20.pdf)
   Use: HUD explanation of Section 18 relocation structure.
12. [HUD SAC Library](https://www.hud.gov/helping-americans/public-indian-housing-sac/library)
   Use: HUD Section 18 program materials and administrative context.

## HACC-Specific Voucher / Hearing / VAWA Duties

13. [42 U.S.C. § 1437d(k)](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?edition=prelim&num=0&req=granuleid%3AUSC-prelim-title42-section1437d)
   Use: grievance / hearing framework for covered public-housing disputes.
14. [24 C.F.R. § 982.555](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-24/part-982/section-982.555)
   Use: informal review / hearing duties in HCV context.
15. [24 C.F.R. § 5.2009](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-24/title-24/subtitle-A/part-5/subpart-L/section-5.2009)
   Use: VAWA emergency-transfer duties.
16. [HUD Form 5380](https://www.hud.gov/sites/default/files/OCHCO/documents/5380.pdf)
   Use: VAWA notice and rights form.
17. [HUD HCV Fair Housing Guidebook, Apr. 2025](https://www.hud.gov/sites/dfiles/PIH/documents/HCV_Guidebook-Chapter_Fair-Housing_April-2025.pdf)
   Use: HCV fair-housing and VAWA administration guidance.

## Oregon Equitable Relief / Specific Performance

18. [Kazlauskas v. Emmert](https://law.justia.com/cases/oregon/court-of-appeals/2012/a141575.html)
   Use: sufficiently definite obligation and inadequacy of legal remedy.
19. [County of Lincoln v. Fischer](https://law.justia.com/cases/oregon/supreme-court/1959/216-or-421-3.html)
   Use: equity where damages are not practical, efficient, and complete.
20. [Pittenger Equip. Co. v. Timber Struct., Inc.](https://law.justia.com/cases/oregon/supreme-court/1950/189-or-1-2.html)
   Use: Oregon specific-performance principles.

## Best Citation Strategy

- Lead with `Winter`, `Alliance`, and `Marlyn`.
- Use FHA authorities as the main joint duty basis against both defendants.
- Use Section 18 authorities as the strongest anti-displacement / relocation-noncompletion basis.
- Use VAWA and hearing authorities as HACC-specific reinforcement.
- Use Oregon specific-performance authorities only as support for preserving final equitable relief, not as a substitute for the federal injunction standard.
