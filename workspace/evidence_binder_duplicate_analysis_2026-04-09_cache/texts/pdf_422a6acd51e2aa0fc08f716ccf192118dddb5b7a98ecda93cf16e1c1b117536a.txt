             Rules, Patterns, and Hybrids
             April 15, 2026
             This session explores a fundamental divide in artificial intelligence between
             rule-based (deterministic) systems and probabilistic, pattern-driven
             approaches. While often assumed or bridged through hybrid methods, this
             distinction is rarely examined directly. The discussion considers how this
             divide not only influences AI development and legaltech strategies, but also
             reflects broader patterns in human cognition and decision-making, including
             traditional jurisprudence.
