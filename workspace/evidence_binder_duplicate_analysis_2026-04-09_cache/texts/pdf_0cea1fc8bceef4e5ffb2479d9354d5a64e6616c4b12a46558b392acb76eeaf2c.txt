   Do you sell bathing suits?

   How warm is the lap pool?
   The lap pool is kept between 80 and 82 degrees.


   How warm is the therapy pool?
   The therapy pool is kept between 90 and 92 degrees.

   How warm is the spa?
   The spa is kept between 101 and 103 degrees.


   Do you have adult swimming lessons?
   Yes, private and semi-private lessons are offered for all abilities fromfear of water to triathlon training.


   Are children allowed to take swim lessons?
   No, the facility is 18 years and older.


   How do I work the pool lift?
   The therapy pool and lap pool lifts are ready for use. For the spa, when you check in at the front desk let
   them know you would like to use the lift. Facilities will get the lift ready.


   Is there a pool only membership?
   No, membership provides access to all areas of the center. It is not possible to restrict access to certain
   areas.


   Do the saline pools have chlorine in them?
   Yes, there is chlorine in the pools. Our saltwater pool has a generator that uses electrolysis to release
   chlorine gas from salt in the water. We use CO2 to treat for pH. Both methods are better for you and
   the environment.


   Why is there white stuff on the bottom of the pools?
   Two things:

     1. Scaling - this is preventative and keeps the buildup of gunk down
     2. Salt deposits

   Are the pools vacuumed?
   The pools are vacuumed overnight on a regular schedule. They are also brushed, backwashed, and
   treated regularly.


   Is the spa cleaned regularly?
   The spa is drained and cleaned once per week. Additional draining may occur based on bather loads
   and chemical composition of the water.


   Do I have to wear a swimsuit while in the pool?
   Yes, swimming suits are required when swimming in the pool. Wearing street clothes or underwear
   including sport bras, compression shorts, basketball shorts and similar clothes are not considered
   proper swim attire and will not be allowed.


   Are towels provided?
   Yes, towels are provided at the front desk (1 small towel and 2 large towels). Please remember to pick
   one up on your way in.
