       development work, Be My Eyes and Meta are proud to provide what promises to
       become game-changing accessibility with hands-free access to the Be My Eyes
       platform.

       Switch seamlessly between smartphone and
       glasses
       Initiate a volunteer call through Be My Eyes on your Meta AI Glasses and toggle t
       o the camera on your smartphone – perhaps to read some fine print or for a high
       resolution wide-angle view – all with a simple double-push of a button. Alternativ

       in Service Directory and toggle to your Meta AI Glasses to continue handsfree.

    More to come
    Developed in partnership with Meta engineers, Be My Eyes is the first and only
    accessibility tech for blind or low vision users available on Meta AI Glasses.
    This first release provides hands-free access to volunteer calls, but our respective
    engineers will continue to evaluate and develop new capabilities over the coming
    months.
