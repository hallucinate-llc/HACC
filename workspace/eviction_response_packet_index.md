## Best Use Note

This packet is designed to be filed inside the eviction case. It is narrower than the federal injunction packet and is built to support:

- denial of possession as premature;
- or, failing that, a stay / abatement and narrow interim preservation relief.
- If the court presses on whether defendants are merely asserting duties, attach or quote [source_linked_duty_matrix_eviction_response.md](/home/barberb/HACC/workspace/source_linked_duty_matrix_eviction_response.md).
