# Proposed Order On Defendants' Motion For Joinder Of Quantum Residential Property Management, L.P.

IN THE CIRCUIT COURT OF THE STATE OF OREGON
FOR THE COUNTY OF CLACKAMAS

HOUSING AUTHORITY OF CLACKAMAS COUNTY,
Plaintiff,

v.

BENJAMIN JAY BARBER and JANE KAY CORTEZ,
Defendants.

Case No. __________________

PROPOSED ORDER ON DEFENDANTS' MOTION FOR JOINDER OF QUANTUM RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY MANAGEMENT, L.P.

The Court, having considered Defendants' Motion for Joinder of Quantum Residential Property Management, L.P., the supporting memorandum, the record, and any argument of the parties, FINDS AND ORDERS as follows:

1. Defendants' Motion for Joinder of Quantum Residential Property Management, L.P. is `GRANTED`.

2. Quantum Residential Property Management, L.P. shall be joined as a party to this action for purposes of resolving the intake-processing, application-routing, replacement-housing, and related relief issues already raised in this case.

3. Within `7 days` of entry of this Order, counsel for Plaintiff or, if applicable, Defendants shall file and serve the operative joinder or third-party pleading naming Quantum Residential Property Management, L.P.

4. Within `14 days` after service, Quantum Residential Property Management, L.P. shall file a response or other permitted appearance.

5. Quantum Residential Property Management, L.P. shall preserve all records, communications, intake logs, scanned materials, application materials, status notes, and internal routing records concerning:
   a. the household's Blossom or Hillside Manor application materials;
   b. any packet, document, or intake materials submitted through the Hillside Manor leasing office;
   c. any communications with HACC regarding those materials; and
   d. any review, forwarding, rejection, or nonprocessing decision regarding the same.

6. Nothing in this Order adjudicates the ultimate merits of defendants' federal or state claims or defenses. This Order is limited to party joinder and preservation of the disputed processing record.

7. The Court reserves for later determination any scheduling adjustments needed as a result of joinder.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
