# Housing Duty Crib Sheet

## Core Theme

HACC should not obtain possession while the housing and transfer paths it triggered remain unfinished.

The cleanest encoded rule is:

- `O(HACC, perform the triggered relocation steps)`
- `F(HACC, treat displacement as complete before actual relocation)`

## HACC Duties

### Section 18

- Source:
  - `42 U.S.C. § 1437p`
  - `24 C.F.R. § 970.21`
- Duties:
  - displacement notice
  - comparable housing / replacement-housing path
  - actual and reasonable relocation assistance
  - counseling
  - no completion of displacement before actual relocation

### HACC's Own PBV / RAD Process

- Source:
  - HACC Administrative Plan
  - Blossom page
  - January 8, 2026 notice
  - March 26, 2026 options email
- Duties:
  - administer the Hillside PBV / RAD replacement-housing track
  - administer the Blossom-or-TPV path HACC directed
  - administer the later three-option path coherently
  - do not convert HACC's own unresolved process into tenant default

### Hearing / Review

- Source:
  - `42 U.S.C. § 1437d(k)`
  - `24 C.F.R. § 982.555`
- Duty:
  - provide lawful notice / hearing path for covered adverse actions

### VAWA / Transfer

- Source:
  - `24 C.F.R. § 5.2007(c)`
  - `24 C.F.R. § 5.2009`
- Duties:
  - maintain and use emergency-transfer framework
  - protect confidentiality
  - administer the transfer path lawfully and coherently

## Quantum Duties

## Best Legal Point

This is not a simple tenant-caused failure to move. HACC triggered relocation duties, directed the household into specific replacement-housing paths, and those paths remained unfinished. HACC should not obtain possession while those duties remain materially incomplete.

## Best Narrow Ask

1. deny possession as premature; or
2. stay / abate possession;
3. require a written status statement;
4. preserve the application, voucher, transfer, and relocation path;
5. do not treat displacement as complete before actual relocation.

## If The Judge Pushes Back

`Are you asking for a specific apartment?`

- No. Only preservation of the housing path and no possession while relocation remains incomplete.

`Why does Quantum matter?`

- Because HACC says Quantum had the packet and did not forward it, which places Quantum inside the same intake chain.

`What is the shortest legal rule?`

- HACC had to carry forward the triggered relocation steps before treating displacement as complete.
