# Lost Service Time Worksheet

Use this worksheet to quantify time diverted away from paid, billable, or commercially valuable work.

## Entry Template

- Date or date range:
- Hours lost:
- Task or service that would otherwise have been performed:
- Related project:
  Linux Foundation, Stanford Law School, open-source AI legal software, or service work
- Why the time was lost:
  calls, emails, paperwork, housing search, moving logistics, accommodation disputes, hearing issues
- Rate or value method:
- Estimated value:
- Supporting document:

## Suggested Categories

- Time spent answering HACC or Quantum emails
- Time spent gathering documents for repeated requests
- Time spent handling relocation or moving issues
- Time spent preparing accommodation materials
- Time spent addressing notice or hearing issues
- Time spent recovering from sleep disruption or crisis-related interruption

## Value Methods

- Actual hourly rate previously used for similar services
- Comparable consulting or contract rate
- Internal project rate used for budgeting
- Reasonable estimate explained in declaration testimony

## Reminder

If a specific rate is uncertain, it is still useful to document the time loss first. The rate can be refined later.
