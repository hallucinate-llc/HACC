## 4. Confirm What Not To Overstate

- do not say Quantum is already proven to bear every HACC duty
- do not say Section 18 is a flat anti-eviction statute
- do not say VAWA guaranteed a completed out-of-county move by a fixed deadline
- do not say the trust papers are already proven invalid

## 5. Rehearse Only The Strongest Points

Housing:

- HACC triggered relocation duties
- HACC directed the household into specific paths
- HACC admitted a packet-routing failure
- HACC should not get possession while that remained unresolved

Probate:

- coercive writ-based relief needs a clean predicate order and notice basis
- plenary / placement / fee relief is overbroad on this record
- trust / authority papers were referenced without being produced
