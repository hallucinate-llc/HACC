# Collateral Estoppel Workspace

This folder is the staging area for guardianship-related drafting and structured case analysis connected to Jane Cortez, Solomon, Benjamin Barber, and HACC.

This workspace is intentionally split between:
- `drafts/` for petition and motion working drafts
- `knowledge_graph/` for graph artifacts, theorem sheets, and relationship maps
- `evidence_notes/` for source logs, verification targets, and allegation tracking
- `research/` for theory memos, including deontic and collateral-estoppel framing

Working conventions for this folder:
- Treat unverified or contested statements as `alleged`, `asserted_by_client`, `reported`, or `to be confirmed`.
- Tie each important claim to a source, exhibit target, or verification task.
- Keep legal theories separated from factual proof so later filings can be tightened cleanly.
- Reuse prior HACC graph and deontic work where it helps, but do not assume it proves guardianship facts without an actual source.

Immediate objectives in this stage:
- maintain a clean petition-for-guardianship drafting workspace
- preserve a first-pass knowledge graph for Solomon, Jane Cortez, Benjamin Barber, and HACC
- stage deontic theorem links for appointment, service/notice, enforceability, and sanctions logic
- identify where prior orders, service history, and housing-contract interference evidence must be attached
- preserve candidate collateral-estoppel and sanctions issues for later proof development
