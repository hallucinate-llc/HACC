- in the guardianship setting, an already-granted role or role-like authority is bypassed and then defended by issue-preclusion or collateral-estoppel positioning; and
- in the housing setting, an already-granted restraining order is treated as if it can be worked around through collateral conversations with HACC rather than obeyed.
