- The petition alleges Benjamin Barber had recently begun living with her in violation of occupancy terms.
- The petition alleges Benjamin Barber failed to provide housing-related documentation and said courts would have to evict him if needed.
- The petition alleges Benjamin Barber interfered with APS access and welfare checks.
- The petition alleges Solomon Barber sought plenary authority and a writ allowing law enforcement to obtain custody of Jane Cortez and transport her to Providence Milwaukie Hospital or another nearby hospital.

## Procedural points that matter for later analysis

- The petition expressly seeks temporary and indefinite guardianship in the same filing.
- The petition expressly seeks waiver of prior notice under an immediate-danger theory.
- The petition expressly seeks a writ of assistance for law enforcement entry and transport.
- The petition states that no guardian had previously been appointed for Jane Cortez.
- The notice packet states Jane Cortez may object to Solomon Barber specifically and to use of her money for related expenses.
- The objection form appears to reflect that Jane Cortez objected.
