# Housing Duties Fact-Law Encoding

This note encodes the current HACC / Quantum duty picture in a form that can be mapped directly into motions for relief.

It is designed to keep the packet disciplined by separating:

1. the legal source;
2. the factual source;
3. the exact deontic rule;
4. the current issue or breach theory; and
5. the narrow remedy presently supported.

## Key

- `O(actor, act)` = obligation / duty
- `F(actor, act)` = prohibition
- `P(actor, act)` = permission
