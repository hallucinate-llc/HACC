# Federal Complaint Track Proffer Note - 2026-04-08

This note controls how the District of Oregon complaint track should be proffered at the current stage.

## Current support posture

The repository currently supports these narrower propositions:

1. a federal complaint packet exists naming Housing Authority of Clackamas County and Quantum Residential as defendants;
2. the scanned packet shows a visible received stamp beginning `RECVD 31 MAR '26 12:57...`, although the trailing characters are faint;
3. the current scan does not clearly show a filled civil action number;
4. the repository complaint packet supports that the federal track concerns overlapping housing-process, relocation, accommodation, and interference issues.

The repository does not yet contain:

1. a filed docket sheet with an assigned civil action number;
2. a filed assignment notice or minute entry confirming judicial assignment;
3. an admissible court record confirming that the matter was under advisement;
4. a filed order confirming the status of any in forma pauperis application.

## Proper proffer framing now

Until filed docket materials are obtained, use this kind of phrasing:

`Repository materials include a scanned District of Oregon complaint packet naming Housing Authority of Clackamas County and Quantum Residential as defendants in a federal civil-rights complaint track arising from the same housing sequence. The packet shows a visible received stamp, but the current scan does not clearly show a civil action number, and filed docket/assignment records remain requested as supplemental proof.`

If the Judge Mosman point is used at all before docket confirmation, frame it this way:

`Movant additionally proffers, from clerk-relayed information, that the complaint and in forma pauperis application were said to be under advisement; Movant requests leave to supplement with filed docket or assignment records before treating that point as court-record-confirmed.`

## Do not yet proffer as a fully established court-record fact

1. that the federal case has a confirmed civil action number;
2. that the complaint has been formally assigned to a specific judge;
3. that Judge Mosman in particular has been confirmed by filed docket materials;
4. that the matter is definitively under advisement as shown by admissible court records.

## Best use in motions and declarations

At the current stage, use the federal track for:

1. overlap and parallel-forum narrative;
2. issue-narrowing arguments;
3. reservation of the right to supplement with filed docket and assignment records later.

Do not yet use it as:

1. a final preclusion anchor;
2. a fully proved filed-and-assigned federal case status;
3. a basis for quoting court status or judicial assignment without later authentication.
