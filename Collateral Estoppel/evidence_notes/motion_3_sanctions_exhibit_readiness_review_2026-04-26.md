## Bottom Line

The exhibit set is usable, but it should be filed in three layers:

1. Primary evidence: court orders, probate petition, writ, Google Voice / Takeout source records, HACC records, trust records, and signed declarations.
2. Declarations: Benjamin and Jane should authenticate the source records, explain personal knowledge, and connect the exhibits to the disputed allegations.
3. Summary aids: matrices, timelines, proof proffers, and sanctions menus should be used as argument aids, not substitutes for the primary records.

The strongest immediate sanctions packet is the one that asks for disclosure, sworn accounting, privilege log, corrective notice, evidentiary limits, adverse evidentiary presumptions where nonproduction is proved, and fees/costs caused by the contempt. The record is not yet equally strong for every factual allegation in the probate petition, so the motion should distinguish proved facts from proof targets.

## Filing Package Recommendation

### Primary Evidence Exhibits

- Probate petition PDF and relevant highlighted pages.
- Restraining order PDF and any proof of notice/service/admission.
- Writ of assistance PDF and service/certificate pages.
- Google Voice / Google Takeout source exports for each selected impeachment excerpt.
- HACC / Quantum source emails, notices, and household-composition records.
- Trust/inheritance source pages, limited to what the pages actually prove.

### Declaration Exhibits

- Benjamin declaration authenticating his messages, emails, housing submissions, personal history with Jane, and observations from the March 23 inspection.
- Jane declaration confirming lack of authorization, no request to remove Benjamin, Solomon's reported HACC communications, and the March 23 inspection statement.

### Summary / Argument Exhibits

- Petition allegation matrix.
- Google Voice impeachment matrix.
- Allegation-vs-knowledge comparison.
- HACC/Quantum delay timeline.
- Proof proffer.
- Sanctions menu.

## Current Weak Spots To Fix Before Filing

1. Jane's supplemental declaration needs Jane's confirmed wording and signature before relying on it for Solomon's HACC communications.
2. The trust/inheritance issue needs direct page cites for any exact amount, acreage, timber lease, or disposition claim.
3. The HACC removal/interference theory needs either a direct HACC source document, Solomon admission, or a narrower inference statement. Current record strongly supports that Benjamin was removed and later restored, but the precise actor/source chain still needs production or subpoena proof.
4. Google Voice summaries should be backed by the actual Takeout exports, screenshots, or transcripts with dates, phone numbers, and metadata preserved.
5. The federal relocation authorities should be framed as showing that the probate allegations were legally incomplete and misleading, not as a standalone factual exhibit.

## Best Sanctions Fit From The Exhibit Set

The exhibits best support requesting:

- an order to show cause and sworn accounting of all sources for the probate allegations;
- production of all HACC, APS, police, medical, trust, estate, and housing communications used or omitted in the probate petition;
- a privilege log for anything withheld;
- an order barring Solomon from relying on unproduced records or undisclosed communications to attack the restraining order;
- an adverse presumption under ORS 40.135 / OEC 311 if the court finds willful suppression or nonproduction;
- corrective notice in probate case `26PR00641` identifying the restraining order and any corrected allegations;
- fees, costs, and other remedial relief caused by the contempt;
- a purgeable daily sanction if Solomon continues to refuse production after a clear compliance order.

The record is weaker for punitive or criminal-style sanctions inside this motion unless the court makes additional findings after hearing. The cleanest ask is remedial, coercive, corrective, and evidentiary.
