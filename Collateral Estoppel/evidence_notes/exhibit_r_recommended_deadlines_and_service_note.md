# Exhibit R Recommended Deadlines And Service Note

This note supplies conservative placeholder guidance for the remaining live fields in the nonparty HACC `Exhibit R` packet.

## Recommended production deadline language

For the motion and proposed order, the cleanest placeholder is:

- `within 14 days of service of the subpoena, unless otherwise ordered by the Court`

If a faster schedule is needed because a hearing or motion depends on the records, a shorter requested deadline can be stated, but the safer default working placeholder is 14 days.

## Recommended objection language

For the proposed order, the cleanest placeholder is:

- `any objection, motion to quash, or request for protective limitation shall be filed within 14 days of service, unless otherwise ordered by the Court`

## Service-method placeholder

The packet should keep the service method blank until the actual method is chosen. The best plain placeholder options are:

- `personal service`
- `first-class mail`
- `certified mail`
- `service through counsel`
- `other lawful method`

Use the one that matches actual service, not the one that sounds strongest.

## Signing / contact placeholders still open

The remaining open items are:

- Benjamin Barber phone number
- Benjamin Barber email
- actual service date
- actual service method
- actual return or response deadline if the court sets one

## Best current use

Use this note only as a packet-completion aid. It does not override court rules, subpoena rules, or any judicial preference on deadlines.
