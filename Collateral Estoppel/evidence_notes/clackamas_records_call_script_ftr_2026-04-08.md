## Call opening

Say:

`Hello, I am trying to submit Request for Copy of FTR Recording forms for several Clackamas County protective-order matters, and I want to make sure I have the correct hearing date, judge, and courtroom number for each case before I submit and pay for the requests.`

If needed, add:

`I am not asking for legal advice. I only need the hearing-identification details required for the FTR request form.`

## Information to ask for on each case

For each matter, ask for:

1. exact case caption;
2. case number;
3. date of the hearing or ruling to request;
4. judge or pro tem judge name;
5. courtroom number;
6. whether there was more than one hearing date that would require separate audio requests;
7. whether the ruling was made at a hearing, ex parte appearance, or later proceeding.

### 1. Benjamin Jay Barber v. Ashley Ferron

`First, I need the hearing details for Benjamin Jay Barber v. Ashley Ferron, case number 26P000433. Can you confirm the hearing date, judge, courtroom, and whether there was more than one proceeding for that matter?`

### 2. Jane Kay Cortez v. Ashley Ferron

Say:

`Second, I need the hearing details for Jane Kay Cortez v. Ashley Ferron, case number 26P000432. Can you confirm the hearing date, judge, courtroom, and whether there was more than one proceeding for that matter?`

### 3. Jane Kay Cortez v. Solomon Samuel Barber

Say:

`Third, I need the hearing details for Jane Kay Cortez v. Solomon Samuel Barber, case number 25PO11530. Can you confirm the hearing date, judge, courtroom, and whether there were multiple proceedings or a later opposition hearing?`

### 4. Jane Kay Cortez v. Julio Cortez

Say:

`Fourth, I need the hearing details for Jane Kay Cortez v. Julio Cortez. I do not yet have the clean case number in front of me. Can you locate it by caption and confirm the case number, hearing date, judge, and courtroom?`

### 5. Benjamin Barber v. Julio Cortez

Say:

`Fifth, I need the hearing details for Benjamin Barber v. Julio Cortez, case number 25PO11318. I have a workspace note that the continued hearing may have been on December 9, 2025, but I would like the clerk's confirmation of the exact date, judge, and courtroom.`

## Follow-up questions

If the clerk confirms multiple hearing dates for any matter, ask:

`Which hearing date would I need to request if I want the audio that contains the court's explanation of the ruling?`

If the clerk says the file was handled by a pro tem judge, ask:

`Can you confirm the full name of the pro tem judge as it appears in the court record so I can put it on the request form?`

If the clerk says there is no audio for a proceeding, ask:

`Is there another record of that ruling, such as minutes, a register entry, or a written order I should request instead?`

## What to write down during the call

For each case, record:

1. confirmed case caption;
2. confirmed case number;
3. confirmed hearing date;
4. confirmed judge name;
5. confirmed courtroom number;
6. whether there are multiple hearing dates;
7. which hearing date is most likely to contain the court's explanation of the ruling;
8. any note from the clerk about no-audio or alternate records.
