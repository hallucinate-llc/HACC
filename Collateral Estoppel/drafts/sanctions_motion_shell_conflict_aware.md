# MOTION FOR SANCTIONS / CONTEMPT - CONFLICT-AWARE SHELL

This draft shell preserves a sanctions pathway without overclaiming unresolved facts.

## A. Core proposition

Sanctions or contempt relief is requested only if the Court finds:
- a valid enforceable order existed;
- the targeted actor had notice (or appeared/evasion is proven);
- prohibited interference or noncompliance occurred;
- the conduct caused prejudice to protected interests (including housing-process harm if proven).

## C. Draft pleading language (safe form)

- "Movant alleges that [ACTOR NAME], after notice of an enforceable order, engaged in conduct that interfered with court-authorized protections and processes."
- "Movant further alleges that this conduct materially disrupted housing or placement actions involving Jane Kay Cortez."
- "These allegations are supported in part by Exhibits __ through __ and will be supplemented by certified docket records."

## D. Evidence thresholds before filing as factual claim

1. attach signed operative order(s) with date and scope
2. attach notice/service/appearance records
3. attach event evidence tying actor to noncompliance/interference
4. attach prejudice evidence (housing delay, placement disruption, financial/property effects)
