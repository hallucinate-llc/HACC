# HACC Hillside Project Records Request

This draft is a targeted records-request version of the Hillside Manor project-agreement demand. It is designed for adaptation into a public-records request, ORCP 55 subpoena attachment, or motion-to-compel exhibit.

## Request objective

Please produce records sufficient to identify the project-side agreements, management structure, and intake / relocation responsibilities governing `Hillside Manor`, `Blossom`, and related RAD / PBV replacement-housing administration for Hillside relocation households.

## Why this request is specific

Public HACC materials already identify `Hillside Manor Apartments` as a RAD / PBV project, identify `Quantum Residential Property Management` as the property manager, and reference:

1. a `06/01/2020 HAP contract`;
2. a `01/01/2021 RAD HAP contract`;
3. a `RAD Use Agreement`;
4. a formal public procurement entry titled `Request for Proposals Management of Hillside Manor`, dated `1/16/2020`, with `Intent to Award` published `2/18/20`; and
5. a public procurement entry titled `Debt and LIHTC Equity Investor RFP – Hillside Manor`, published `5/29/19`, with linked materials including `Financials`, `Addendum 1`, `Rental Assistance Demonstration Physical Condition Assessment (RPCA)`, and `Intent to Award`.

## Requested records

1. The fully executed `06/01/2020 HAP contract` for Hillside Manor.
2. The fully executed `01/01/2021 RAD HAP contract` for Hillside Manor.
3. The initial and any amended `RAD Use Agreement` for Hillside Manor.
4. Any fully executed `management agreement`, property-management services agreement, or equivalent contract identifying Quantum Residential's role at Hillside Manor.
5. Any fully executed `ground lease`, `control agreement`, development agreement exhibit, regulatory agreement, ownership agreement, or equivalent governance instrument for Hillside Manor.
6. The complete file for `Request for Proposals Management of Hillside Manor`, dated `1/16/2020`, including:
   - the RFP;
   - all addenda;
   - bid or proposal submissions sufficient to identify the selected manager;
   - scoring sheets;
   - evaluation memoranda;
   - recommendation memoranda; and
   - the `Intent to Award` published `2/18/20`.
7. The complete file for `Debt and LIHTC Equity Investor RFP – Hillside Manor`, published `5/29/19`, including:
   - the RFP;
   - `Financials`;
   - `Addendum 1`;
   - `Rental Assistance Demonstration Physical Condition Assessment (RPCA)`;
   - proposal evaluations;
   - recommendation memoranda; and
   - `Intent to Award`.
8. All records sufficient to identify the ownership and control structure for Hillside Manor after RAD conversion, including:
   - the owner;
   - any HACC-related entity;
   - any tax-credit partnership;
   - the management entity; and
   - any party responsible for PBV contract administration.
9. All records sufficient to identify which party or parties were responsible for:
   - application intake;
   - routing of relocation packets;
   - PBV waitlist or project-based voucher processing;
   - leasing coordination;
   - tenant communications; and
   - preservation of application materials for Hillside relocation households.
10. All policies, riders, exhibits, attachments, or incorporated documents addressing:
   - RAD requirements;
   - PBV requirements;
   - Section 18 replacement housing;
   - grievance / hearing procedures;
   - relocation implementation; and
   - tenant selection / priority rules for Hillside relocation households.
11. All records sufficient to identify whether `Blossom`, `Hillside Manor`, and `Parkside Heights` were part of the same or overlapping Hillside Manor redevelopment / relocation implementation chain.
12. All records sufficient to identify whether Quantum Residential, Hillside Manor staff, Blossom staff, or Parkside Heights staff received, routed, held, or failed to forward relocation or application packets for Jane Cortez / Benjamin Barber.
