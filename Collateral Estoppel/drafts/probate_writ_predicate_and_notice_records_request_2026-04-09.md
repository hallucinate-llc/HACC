# Probate Writ Predicate And Notice Records Request

This draft is a targeted court-records or subpoena-style request for the filings, orders, and notice materials underlying the writ-based forced-transport sequence in case `26PR00641`.

It is designed for adaptation into a certified-records request, ORCP 55 subpoena attachment, or motion-to-compel exhibit.

## Request objective

Please produce the complete predicate-order and notice record supporting any writ of assistance, law-enforcement-assisted transport, forced-entry, or comparable coercive removal relief sought or issued in case `26PR00641`.

## Why this request is specific

The current record already includes a probate-side writ of assistance directing law enforcement to accompany Solomon Barber, enter Jane Kay Cortez's residence forcibly if necessary, locate her, and transport her to Providence Milwaukie Hospital or another nearby hospital.

The present request seeks the specific records needed to determine:

1. what order authorized that writ;
2. what notice basis existed;
3. what emergency findings were actually made;
4. what filings counsel submitted in support of the writ; and
5. what service or post-appointment notice record exists.

## Requested records

1. The signed order appointing any temporary guardian, temporary fiduciary, guardian ad litem, or other authority figure whose authority was relied on for the writ.
2. The signed order, minute order, or other signed judicial authorization specifically supporting issuance of the writ of assistance or comparable coercive transport/removal relief.
3. The full application, motion, petition, affidavit, declaration, certificate of readiness, and any attached proposed order used to seek the writ.
4. The register, docket entries, or case chronology sufficient to show:
   - when the writ-related filings were filed;
   - when any temporary appointment order was entered;
   - when the writ was issued;
   - when any hearing was set or held; and
   - when any proof of service or notice was filed.
5. All notice, service, waiver-of-notice, or emergency-notice materials concerning:
   - Jane Kay Cortez;
   - Benjamin Barber;
   - Solomon Samuel Barber; and
   - any counsel appearing in the matter.
6. Any findings, affidavits, or declarations asserting immediate and serious danger, need for waiver of notice, or justification for ex parte relief.
7. Any records sufficient to identify whether pre-appointment notice was waived, and if so, on what factual and legal basis.
8. Any proof of service, certificate of mailing, sheriff return, or other post-issuance notice/service material relating to the writ or predicate temporary-guardian order.
9. Any communication or instruction to law enforcement transmitted with the writ, including cover sheets, transmission logs, or related directive documents.
10. Any filed notice, motion, or order showing whether the writ was later dissolved, stayed, modified, or superseded.
