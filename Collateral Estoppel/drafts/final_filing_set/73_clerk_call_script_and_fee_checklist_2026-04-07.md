## Fee/payment checklist

1. Ask if certification fee is per page, per document, or per packet.
2. Ask if additional archive/retrieval fees apply.
3. Ask if payment must be made before processing or before release.
4. Ask what payment channels are accepted (phone card, online, check, in person).
5. Ask what identifying memo/reference should be included with payment.
6. Ask whether a payment receipt number must be sent back to the clerk.
7. Ask whether expedited processing is available and the cost.
